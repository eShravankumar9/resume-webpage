About me

Inspiration and Passion

Imagine a curious young boy, wide-eyed and full of questions, marveling at the unique color of the Statue of Liberty. That boy was me, and little did I know, that simple question would ignite a lifelong passion for tackling global challenges.

As I eagerly asked my father about the statue's distinct hue, he shared with me the fascinating world of corrosion—the unseen force that shapes our world in profound ways. Learning that corrosion costs the world an astonishing $2.5 trillion annually was eye-opening, to say the least.

by this revelation, I embarked on a journey to understand the profound impact of corrosion on our world. With each story of monumental structures succumbing to the relentless march of rust, including the indomitable Eiffel Tower, my fascination grew.Fueled by this newfound passion, I pursued a Bachelor's degree in Metallurgy and Materials Engineering.

Educational Journey and Career Transition

Throughout my studies, I delved into courses spanning from Computer Programming and Data Structures to Metallurgical Analysis and Computation. It was during these transformative years that I discovered the transformative power of programming to address complex challenges and drive innovation.

To delve deeper into the realm of technology, I set my sights on the software industry. In my third year, I seized an opportunity to interview with Tata Consultancy Services (TCS), a renowned leader in IT services. To my delight, I was offered a position as a software developer—a pivotal moment that marked the beginning of my journey in harnessing technology to combat the world's most pressing challenges.
